Known Issues

Critical

  • Magnetic SDF coupling still unstable: permanent/soft magnetic SDF objects can still overdrive the ferro solve. Cursor-only magnetic scenes are much more stable now, so the remaining failure likely lives in the rigid-SDF magnetic source/coupling path.
  • Firecracker/Reactive Burn bottom-left drift: P2G burst direction is random+symmetric, so the bias likely comes from the Euler air field or grid boundary conditions. The MAC grid may have a slight asymmetry from the SDF boundary enforcement or the pressure solver iteration order.
  • Material collapse under gravity: All materials share global E (currently 40000). Materials that compute E_g = E * multiplier may still be too soft if multiplier < 1.0 or if the stress clamp (0.8E) limits the elastic response.

Moderate

  • Burning duration too short: Vapor system's latent_cooling may drain heat faster than exothermic generation. Some materials cool before they can propagate fire.
  • Per-particle E not stored: Preset E values are cosmetic — all particles use global E. Need SSBO for per-particle base_params.
  • Structural charring subtle: Damage field color changes may be masked by the keep_colors batch mode.
  • Ash buoyancy weak: G2P buoyancy competes with gravity through the MPM grid. Euler air convection helps but coupling is weak.

Architecture

Physics Pipeline (per frame):

MPM (5 substeps: clear→P2G→grid_op→thermal→G2P) → SPH (2 substeps: hash→density→codim→force) → Euler Air (forces→diffuse→div→pressure(20 RBGS)→project→advect→enforce_bc→inject→drag→blow→heat_gun)

Key SSBO Bindings: 0-12 particles, 13-16 spatial hash+codim, 17 SDF prims, 40-46+55 MPM grid, 47-54 MPM extra, 60-72 Euler MAC grid

Shaders: ~40 compute + ~12 vert/frag
